What is EduPilot?

EduPilot is India’s smart education guidance platform—built to help students make confident, interest-based decisions at critical turning points in their academic journey. Whether you're in Class 10 choosing your stream or in Class 12 planning your career, EduPilot acts as your personal co-pilot, guiding you through options, entrance exams, and future goals.

Why Use EduPilot?

    Personalized Guidance
  • Match your interests, strengths, and goals to the right stream or career
  • Get tailored suggestions for medical, engineering, law, design, commerce, and more
    Stream & Career Selector
  • Interactive tools to help you choose between Science, Commerce, and Humanities
  • Explore career paths like MBBS, CA, UPSC, IIT, NDA, and BA LLB with clarity
    Entrance Exam Planner
  • Know which exams to prepare for: NEET, JEE, CLAT, CUET, NATA, etc.
  • Get timelines, syllabus breakdowns, and strategy tips
    College & Course Explorer
  • Discover top colleges across India based on your stream and goals
  • Learn about scholarships, reservation policies, and government scheme
    Real Voices, Real Stories
  • Hear from students, professionals, and mentors across India
  • Regional language support for deeper accessibility
    India-Focused, Future-Ready
  • Covers CBSE, ICSE, State Boards, and Open Schooling
  • Designed for Indian students with Indian career realities in mind

Our Mission

To empower students across India with clear, personalized guidance on choosing the right stream after Class 10 and the right career path after Class 12—based on interests, aptitude, and real-world opportunities.
